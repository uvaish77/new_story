INTRODUCTION:

CARD Trust 100-bed Cancer care and prevention project is to provide Cancer patients and the chronically/terminally ill people with easily accessible quality healthcare services through integration of oncology and palliative care.

THE HOSPITAL SEEKS TO PROVIDE SPECIALTY SERVICES:

  • Medical, Surgical & Radiation Oncology, Palliative Care, Paediatric Cancer, Pain Management & Support Services
  • A state of the art Lab for Bio-chemistry, Microbiology, Pathology and Haematology will also form part of the Hospital.
  • A state of the art Lab for Bio-chemistry, Microbiology, Pathology and Haematology will also form part of the Hospital.
  • A separate Geriatric – Male and Female Paediatric wards.

    • ABOUT THE PROJECT:

  • Construction of a day-care block and a 100 Bed Hospital
  • Offer affordable and quality services
  • Estimated built-up area of about 70,000 Square Feet on 10 acres
  • Land estimated total project cost of INR 150 Crores.
  • Will cater 10 Out-Patient Consultation Rooms, 4 Operation Theatres and 100 In-Patient Beds of which 28 will be transit beds and 72 operational beds

    • To be completed and ready for operations within two years from the commencement of the project; the OPD block will however, be operational within 8-10 months.

    The hospital will be an Exclusive Modernized and well-equipped Cancer care Super-Specialty. It will provide comprehensive services to cover all the health care needs of the Cancer patients. The facilities includes spacious green cover, alternative treatments such as yoga, pranic healing, music therapy, etc.
